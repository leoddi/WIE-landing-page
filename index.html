Description

A platform designed to provide users with insights and analytics about their listening habits on Spotify. By integrating with the Spotify API, users can securely connect their Spotify accounts to the web application and gain access to various features and functionalities that analyze their listening data. The application aims to offer users a personalized and insightful experience, helping them understand their music preferences, discover new artists and genres, and track their listening trends over time.

Functionalities

Seamless Spotify Integration

Connect your Spotify account securely to unlock a world of music analytics tailored just for you.

Personalized Insights

Gain valuable insights into your music habits, preferences, and trends, all presented in a user-friendly dashboard.

Track Listening Trends

Keep tabs on your listening trends over time, tracking changes in your music preferences and discovering emerging patterns.

Genre Exploration

Delve into the diverse world of music genres, understanding your affinity towards different styles and artists.
